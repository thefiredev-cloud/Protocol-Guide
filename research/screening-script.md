# Participant Screening Script

## Overview

Use this script to screen potential research participants before scheduling interviews or ride-alongs. Screening takes 3-5 minutes.

---

## Screening Criteria

### Must Have (Qualifying)
- Currently certified EMT, AEMT, or Paramedic
- Actively working in EMS (responded to calls in past 30 days)
- Uses some form of protocol reference (not 100% memory)

### Nice to Have (For Diversity)
- Mix of experience levels (0-2 years, 3-5 years, 5+ years)
- Mix of service types (fire, private, hospital, volunteer)
- Mix of geography (urban, suburban, rural)
- Mix of genders
- Mix of device usage (iPhone, Android, tablet)

### Disqualifying Factors
- Not currently certified
- Works in EMS education/administration only (no field work)
- Has not responded to calls in past 30 days
- Works for competitor protocol app company

---

## Screening Script

### Introduction

> Hi [NAME], thank you for your interest in participating in our research study. I have a few quick questions to make sure this study is a good fit for you. This will only take about 3 minutes.

### Screening Questions

**Q1. Are you currently certified as an EMT, AEMT, or Paramedic?**
- If YES: Continue
- If NO: "Thank you for your interest, but this study requires active EMS certification. We appreciate your time."

**Q2. What is your certification level?**
- [ ] EMT / EMT-Basic
- [ ] Advanced EMT (AEMT)
- [ ] Paramedic (EMT-P)
- [ ] Other: _______________

**Q3. Are you currently working in EMS? Have you responded to emergency calls in the past 30 days?**
- If YES to both: Continue
- If NO: "Thank you for your interest, but this study focuses on active field providers. We appreciate your time."

**Q4. How long have you been working in EMS?**
- [ ] Less than 1 year
- [ ] 1-2 years
- [ ] 3-5 years
- [ ] 6-10 years
- [ ] More than 10 years

**Q5. What type of EMS service do you work for?**
- [ ] Fire department based
- [ ] Private ambulance service
- [ ] Hospital-based EMS
- [ ] Third service (city/county government)
- [ ] Volunteer
- [ ] Other: _______________

**Q6. How would you describe your primary response area?**
- [ ] Urban (large city)
- [ ] Suburban
- [ ] Rural
- [ ] Frontier/wilderness

**Q7. How do you currently access medical protocols when you need them?**
- [ ] Printed binder/book
- [ ] PDF on phone/tablet
- [ ] Protocol app - which one? _______________
- [ ] Mostly from memory
- [ ] Other: _______________

**Q8. Do you work for or have any affiliation with a company that makes EMS protocol software or apps?**
- If YES: "Thank you for your interest, but we're unable to include participants with industry affiliations. We appreciate your time."
- If NO: Continue

---

## Qualification Decision

Based on responses, participant is:

- [ ] **QUALIFIED** - Schedule for interview
- [ ] **QUALIFIED** - Schedule for ride-along observation (additional criteria met)
- [ ] **QUALIFIED** - Add to survey distribution list only
- [ ] **NOT QUALIFIED** - Thank and exit

### Additional Criteria for Ride-Along

If participant expressed interest in ride-along AND meets all of the following:
- [ ] Works for agency willing to host researchers
- [ ] Can accommodate 8-12 hour observation
- [ ] Located within reasonable travel distance

## Scheduling Script

### For Qualified Participants - Interview

> Great news - you qualify for our study! We'd like to schedule a 45-60 minute interview with you.

> The interview will be conducted via video call and will focus on how you access and use medical protocols in the field. As a thank you, you'll receive a $75 Amazon gift card within 5 business days of completing the interview.

> What times typically work best for you?
> [Offer 3-4 specific time slots]

> What email address should I send the calendar invite to?

> Do you have any questions before we wrap up?

### For Qualified Participants - Ride-Along Interest

> Based on what you've shared, you might also be a great candidate for our ride-along observation study. This would involve a researcher joining you for a shift to observe how you access protocols in real-world conditions.

> A few quick questions:
> - Would your agency be open to hosting a researcher for a ride-along?
> - Who would we need to contact for approval?
> - What's your typical shift length?

> This is a separate study with a $100 gift card incentive, plus a donation to your agency. Would you be interested?
