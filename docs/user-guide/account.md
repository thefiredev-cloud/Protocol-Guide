# Account & Subscription

Manage your Protocol Guide account, settings, and subscription.

---

## Account Management

### Creating an Account

Protocol Guide uses secure single sign-on (SSO):

1. Tap **"Sign In"** on the home screen
2. Choose your provider:
   - **Sign in with Google** (Gmail account)
   - **Sign in with Apple** (Apple ID)
3. Authorize the app when prompted
4. Your account is created automatically

### Why Sign In?

Without an account:
- ✅ Basic search functionality
- ❌ No search history sync
- ❌ No bookmarks
- ❌ Limited to device

With an account:
- ✅ Full search functionality
- ✅ Search history saved
- ✅ Bookmarks sync (Pro)
- ✅ Settings sync across devices
- ✅ Subscription management

### Account Security

- **No passwords stored**: Uses secure OAuth
- **Two-factor authentication**: Through Google/Apple
- **Session management**: View active sessions in settings

**Includes**:
- ✅ Basic protocol search
- ✅ Voice input
- ✅ Search history (local)
- ✅ Offline mode (essential protocols)

- ✅ Priority support
- ✅ Early access to new features

**Best for**: Active EMS professionals

## Upgrading to Pro

### From the App

1. Go to **Profile** → **Subscription**
2. Tap **"Upgrade to Pro"**
3. Choose your plan:
   - Monthly: $4.99/month
   - Annual: $39/year (save 35%)
4. Complete payment with Stripe
5. Pro features activate immediately

### Payment Methods

- Credit/Debit Card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Apple Pay (when available)
- Google Pay (when available)

### Billing

- **Monthly**: Billed on the same day each month
- **Annual**: Billed once per year
- **Receipts**: Sent to your email after each payment

## Managing Your Subscription

### View Subscription Status

Go to **Profile** → **Subscription**

You'll see:
- Current plan (Free/Pro/Enterprise)
- Billing cycle
- Next payment date
- Payment method

### Update Payment Method

1. Go to **Profile** → **Subscription** → **Payment Method**
2. Tap **"Update"**
3. Enter new card details
4. Save

### Cancel Subscription

1. Go to **Profile** → **Subscription**
2. Tap **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Confirm cancellation

**What happens when you cancel**:
- You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period
- After that, you revert to Free tier
- Your bookmarks remain but won't sync
- Search history is preserved

### Reactivate Subscription

If you've cancelled, you can reactivate anytime:

1. Go to **Profile** → **Subscription**
2. Tap **"Reactivate Pro"**
3. Pro features restore immediately

## Profile Settings

### Edit Profile

Go to **Profile** → **Edit Profile**

**You can change**:
- Display name
- Profile photo
- Preferred region/county

**You cannot change** (managed by your sign-in provider):
- Email address

### Notification Settings

Go to **Profile** → **Settings** → **Notifications**

| Notification | Default | Description |
|--------------|---------|-------------|
| Protocol Updates | ON | When your region's protocols change |
| Product News | OFF | New features and updates |
| Tips & Tutorials | OFF | Usage tips |

### App Preferences

Go to **Profile** → **Settings** → **Preferences**

**Display**:
- Theme: Auto/Light/Dark
- Text size: Small/Medium/Large
- High contrast mode: On/Off

**Search**:
- Auto-submit voice search: On/Off
- Show related searches: On/Off
- Search result detail level: Compact/Full

**Accessibility**:
- Screen reader support: On/Off
- Reduce motion: On/Off
- Haptic feedback: On/Off

## Region/County Selection

### Why Select a Region?

Protocol Guide customizes results based on your region:
- Shows protocols specific to your medical direction
- Prioritizes your local guidelines
- Displays your agency's drug formulary

### Changing Your Region

1. Go to **Profile** → **Settings** → **Region**
2. Search for your new county/region
3. Tap to select
4. Confirm the change

### Multiple Regions

If you work in multiple areas:
- Your primary region is used by default
- Switch regions quickly from the search screen
- Tap the region indicator to change

## Data & Privacy

### What We Collect

| Data Type | Purpose | Retention |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Email | Account identification | Until account deletion |
| Search queries | Improve results, history | Until you clear |
| Usage analytics | Improve the product | Anonymized, aggregated |

### What We Don't Collect

- ❌ Patient information
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Audio recordings (processed, not stored)
- ❌ Personal health information

### Export Your Data

Request a copy of your data:

1. Go to **Profile** → **Settings** → **Privacy**
2. Tap **"Export My Data"**
3. You'll receive an email with your data within 48 hours

### Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your account and all data:

1. Go to **Profile** → **Settings** → **Privacy**
2. Tap **"Delete Account"**
3. Confirm by entering your email
4. Account and all data are deleted within 30 days

## Device Management

### View Logged-In Devices

Go to **Profile** → **Settings** → **Security** → **Devices**

See all devices where you're signed in:
- Device type (iPhone, Android, etc.)
- Last active
- Location (approximate)

### Sign Out of Devices

**Sign out of a specific device**:
1. Find the device in the list
2. Tap **"Sign Out"**

**Sign out of all devices**:
1. Tap **"Sign Out All Devices"**
2. You'll need to sign in again everywhere
