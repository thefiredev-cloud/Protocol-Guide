## Search History

### Automatic History

Every search you perform is automatically saved:

- **Query text**: What you searched for
- **Timestamp**: When you searched
- **Results**: The AI response (for offline access)

### Viewing History

1. Tap the **search bar**
2. Tap the **clock icon** (ğŸ•)
3. See your recent searches

Or:
1. Go to **Profile** â†’ **Search History**
2. Browse all historical searches

### Using History

**Re-run a Search:**
- Tap any previous search to run it again

**View Cached Results:**
- Previous results are stored offline
- Tap to view without re-searching

**Delete History Items:**
1. Swipe left on any item
2. Tap **"Delete"**

### Clearing All History

To delete all search history:

1. Go to **Profile** â†’ **Settings** â†’ **Privacy**
2. Tap **"Clear Search History"**
3. Confirm the action

## Privacy Considerations

### What's Stored

- **Locally**: Search queries, results, bookmarks
- **Synced (Pro)**: Bookmarks, preferences
- **Not Stored**: Patient information, voice audio

### Data Retention

- Search history: Until you delete it
- Bookmarks: Until you delete them
- Cached protocols: Until you clear cache or delete app

### Clearing Your Data

Go to **Profile** â†’ **Settings** â†’ **Privacy**:

- **Clear Search History**: Removes all past searches
- **Clear Bookmarks**: Removes all saved protocols
- **Clear All Data**: Factory reset of app data
