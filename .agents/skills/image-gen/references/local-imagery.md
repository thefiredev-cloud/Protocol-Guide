# Australian-Specific Imagery

Details to include for authenticity in Australian contexts.

## Landmarks by Region

### Sydney
- Harbour Bridge, Opera House, Bondi Beach
- CBD skyline, Darling Harbour
- Federation-style terraces

### Melbourne
- Federation Square, Yarra River, St Kilda
- Flinders Street Station, laneways
- Victorian-era architecture

### Brisbane
- Story Bridge, South Bank, City Botanic Gardens
- Queenslander houses

### Newcastle
- Nobby's Lighthouse, Newcastle Beach
- Fort Scratchley, Hunter River
- Industrial heritage

### Regional NSW
- Hunter Valley vineyards
- Blue Mountains
- Murray River, Snowy Mountains

## Australian Architecture

subtropical garden. Queensland vernacular.

```
Red brick house with decorative timber fretwork,

```

### Modern Australian
```
Contemporary home with large windows,
indoor-outdoor flow, timber decking,
native garden, clean lines.
```

## Flora

Include for authenticity:
- Eucalyptus / gum trees (distinctive bark, blue-green leaves)
- Banksia (iconic flower spikes)
- Wattle / acacia (golden yellow)
- Native grasses
- Kangaroo paw
- Grevillea

## Seasonal Considerations

Australian seasons are reversed from Northern Hemisphere:
- Summer: December - February (Christmas at beach!)
- Autumn: March - May
- Winter: June - August
- Spring: September - November

For seasonal imagery:
- Summer: Beach, outdoor dining, bright sun
- Winter: Cozy cafe, fireplace, rain
